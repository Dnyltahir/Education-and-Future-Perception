About Me

Hello, I'm Sher Abbas Aliyar, a multimedia designer and visual storyteller from Afghanistan, currently studying Communication and Design. My work combines design, photography, video, and research to explore social issues and create meaningful experiences.

I believe design can do more than look good. It can educate, raise awareness, and inspire positive change. Through my projects, I focus on understanding people's experiences and creating solutions that are simple, engaging, and impactful.

This thesis project reflects my passion for using design as a tool to support education and encourage positive change in society.
